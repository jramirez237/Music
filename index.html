Biography:

Myke Towers, a Puerto-Rican rapper and singer, was born January 15, 1994 and is currently 30 years old. He began his singing career in 2016 and up to now has collborated with many artists: Bad Bunny, Becky G, Piso 21, and many more. His first album was "Easy Money Baby", his second was "Lyke Mike", and his third one was "La Vida Es Una" which is his most recent album relased in March of 2023.
